Automation Project 3 Tamper seal Applicator

The tamper seal automation machine kept malfuntioning and would either damage boxes if its ridgid applicator bar was too snug, or not perform any seal at all if too loose. So I custom made a flexible applicator bar that reduced the incident rate to 1 out of every 1000 seals vs the 1 out of every 10 that was occuring before the addition.

Lactate Sensor

In this project I made a working lactate sensor with the end goal of developing a continuous lactate monitor so athletes can track how much lactic acid they produce. We were able to use sensor principles to create a circuit that converted the current produced from lactate oxidase reacting with lactate similar to glucose oxidase in glucose sensors. We then used our circuit with a potentiostat and transimpedance amplifier to make a measurable voltage that varied with the amount of lactate that was added to the system. We then made a lactate calibration curve that represented how much voltage gets produced depending on the lactate concentration in the sweat.
